_Kneeboard_
Separate kneeboard chapters by blank pages, to place the starting point of each at multiples of ten pages.
Then say "Kneeboard 10 Forward" to move chapter to chapter.
Mark and jump shortcuts can be used flexibly from there.



_Nonstandard Keys_
LShift+K	Make Kneeboard Shortcut
LAlt+LWin+1	Kneeboard Jump To Shortcut 1

Slider		Radar Elevation

Thumbstick	Thrust Designator Control (TDC)

ChaffOff/FlareOn, FlareOff, ChaffOn

-F5E-
LCtrl+F		Flaps Switch Step Aft
LShift+F	Flaps Switch Step Fwd
